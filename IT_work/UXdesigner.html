UX designer









This website describes an overview, career path, the salary of UX designer with various resources and suggests several job opportunities. According to the website, the role of UX designers is to improve usability and accessibility between user and products. They state that a degree is not necessary and limited in a specific major to become a UX designer, but also highlight it will probably be useful if applicants are accustomed to handling code because it will help communicate with developers.









In this video, UX designer whose name is Ryan explains the motivation to become a designer and main duties of the designer by revealing his daily routine. He believes that his works tend to be flexible and varied. He strongly highlights that the overall tasks of UX designer are to interact with clients, colleagues as well as developers, so communication competence is a critical skill for UX designer. He also makes efforts to study design trends constantly and build his own design to produce efficient interfaces.





Description









The role of a UX designer may be regarded as a bridge between developers teams and customers. The major duties of the UX designer are to analyze user experiences and design interfaces of an application or product. Specifically, they usually devote their time investigating how customers feel about their products by conducting interviews or surveys.









They are also responsible for considering which design is the most effective to express the feature of the product and the most appropriate for their expected customer. They are likely to work with people in various fields such as developers and clients because the UX designer should consider the limitation of implementing their design technically with developers and create designs which meet user expectations. Therefore, it is anticipated that seeking a balance between the limitation of products and user expectation is one of the challenges of UX designers.
