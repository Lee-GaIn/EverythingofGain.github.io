Tools and Technologies





The project mainly requires three tools and technologies which are Speech to text & Text to speech software, artificial intelligence and application making programs. The first demanded technology is Speech to text & Text to speech. STT means recognizing a user's voice and converting user speech to text, vice versa TTS refers to converting text to voice by using machine voice. The well-known examples of this are Siri, Google Docs Voice Typing and Amazon Lex Next. The AI is also required. AI is a machine programmed to mimic human behaviour and learn itself from new experiences. AI has been applied in many services, specifically riding apps like uber. Lastly, the service will be offered by the application. Nowadays, most people tend to use phones rather than computers, so it is necessary to offer services in various types to enhance accessibility.
