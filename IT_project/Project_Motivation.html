Motivation





After industrialization, human lifestyle became more comfortable than prehistoric time. Most people are guaranteed daily necessities as well as they can spend their time to enjoy entertainment rather than storing and gathering food the whole day. This change has many advantages such as saving time, but it also has disadvantages regarding health. For instance, the development of transportation and new entertainment like e-sports leads to lack of activities of daily living in life. According to WHO reports, obesity has approximately tripled since 1975 world globally. It could be considered a global crisis in that serious illness like heart disease and cancer caused by obesity. This project is suggested since obesity is preventable if people keep appropriate food portions, the quality of food and regular exercise.
