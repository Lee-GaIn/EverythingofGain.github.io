Overview







In general, mankind, since the improvement of cookery, eat about twice as much as nature requires. — Benjamin Franklin(1706–1790)


Obesity has become one of the major concerns in the world. 2016, the fact that over 340 million aged 5 to 19 adolescents and children were obese or overweight is presented by WHO. This project will concentrate on improving the user's health considering proper diet and regular exercise. To provide a suitable health plan, artificial intelligence will analyze each user’s current health state and recommend a customized diet and exercise schedules based on the proven report. Moreover, the project will provide a health diary service to let the user document their log related to health and report analysis regularly. The service will be offered as an application and required software like speech recognition. It is expected that illness due to obesity will diminish and average longevity will escalate if the project is performed efficiently.
